# Hello, I'm Youngmin (Jonas) Kim

I'm a **Senior Data Scientist at AWS Professional Services (ProServe) Korea**. For over 15 years I've worked as a data scientist and machine learning engineer, living at the seam between research papers and production systems — turning frontier ideas into things that actually run.

This blog is what I collect along that seam. I follow the weekly flood of LLM, multimodal, and agent research, but the rule here is simple: keep asking *why it matters*, not just what the benchmark says. I care more about the **design decisions and trade-offs** beneath a result than the result itself.

If something here sparks a thought, I'd love to hear it — reach out through any of the channels below.
